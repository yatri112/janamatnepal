Our Activities

Our Activities

  1. Training to the Jurisdiction and Employee and Affiliate:
    • Muluki Ain-2075 and New Legal system of judicial works
    • Sale/Purchase, Consumer, Account, Tax Management and Monitoring
    • Regarding good governance and targeted planning
    • Right to Information and Fundamental Rights
    • Challenges in political leadership- expectations and election competition

  2. Detailed Statistical Survey:
    • History, gender, age, education, health, communication, sanitation, employment, property, physical-economic status

  3. Discussion Symposium:
    • How to coordinate between the province and the local government.
    • How to coordinate between the Central, Province and Local government
    • How to coordinate between local government and indigenous foreigners and donor bodies and diplomatic regulations.
    • Equal rights of Son and Daughter on fathers property, Dowry System management, son-daughter's educational status, marital status, maternal orphanage and behavioral condition
  4. Profile Creating
    • Creating a local level profile, map of road condition, school and map of educational status

  5. Documentry Biography Making:
    • Making Documentary-biography and published on National and International TV channel as well as online media.

About Us

Jamamat Sanchar Kendra (CCPV Nepal) is a public opinion communication center for advocacy and training, with the objective of coordinating the mutual and domestic and foreign donations and mutual coordination between the democratic rules in the protection and consolidation of democracy.
