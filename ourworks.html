Our Works

Main functions:

• Discussion, advocacy and media interaction to be done for backward area, Muslim, Janjati, Adiwasi, Tharu, women, single mothers, senior citizens and differently able people in countries where the HDI is low.

• Advocacy of democratic value recognition and human rights in favor of common citizen.

• The citizen's personal rights are saved, advocate for the freedom of worship, freedom of speech, and right to information.

• To prepare report on the basis of Voting survey, statistical survey as well as ground survey after monitoring.

• To conduct research, training, policy making, capacity building as well as awareness programmes.

• Making documentary, website and digital media operations and social media management.

• To organize, conduct and coordinate the discussion on various issues through seminars, gatherings and conferences.

• To organize code of conduct trainings for the provincial as well as local level representatives and employees.

• Survey on the activity of political parties, the election conditions of the electoral manifesto and the election of the previous political parties.

• To monitor as well as supervise various activities of governmental and non- governmental foreign donor agencies and diplomats and investment programmes with the leading of public hearings.

• To advocate on any other issues related to the activities of different national and international organiations, donor bodies and foreign diplomats.

• To note down subject related matters, expectations, like-dislike for donor agencies or other foreign agencies to have an environment to work as well as to invest.
