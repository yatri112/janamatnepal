About Us

Welcome to  Civil Media And Research Center Established Since 1898

This is a purely social organization Civil Media And Research Center, Nepal (Communication Center Of Public Views, Nepal-CCPV Nepal) a public opinion communication center for advocacy and training, with the objective of coordinating the mutual and domestic and foreign donations and mutual coordination between the democratic rules in the protection and consolidation of democracy. With the above objectives we are working past 7 years in this field, the main office of this organization is in Babarmahal, Kathmandu.

Specially, Regional and Local Level Representative and concerned staff, Capacity Building, Data/Counting Surveys, Statistics Survey, Policy Rule-Producing, Documentary Production and Broadcasting, Digital Office Operations, Website Creation and Social Media Management, High-Level Thinking Summit -This organization actively working on organizing coordination-combinations and coordination.

Besides, various activities of government and non-governmental donor bodies and cultural donations, various activities, monitoring and supervision of investment-monitoring and public hearings, the work they need to do, seek the necessary subjects for investment habits and expectations of targeted class-expectations.
